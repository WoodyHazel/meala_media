About

I grew up always begging to use my parent's camera and finally for one of my birthdays I got my first canon. I think I took about 1,000 photos of just flowers that first month.

Fast forward to college, I really felt a pull to start capturing again but this time I wanted to understand more about how to pose people, settings, and the light. I have had the honor of capturing beautiful humans for about 3-4 years and this year I have fully committed to my photography business.

I adore my clients and I will be celebrating you from the moment you reach out for a shoot. It takes strength and bravery to show up for a shoot, just know that I recognize and validate that. I will be here to help make your shoot memorable, safe, and healing from start to finish.

Outside of photography, I have my Masters of Social work with a mental health emphasis, I am a birth/postpartum doula, and I am Key leader at Lululemon.
